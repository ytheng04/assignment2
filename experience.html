My Experience Timeline

2024

My first interview. I prepared a lot of questions that might be asked, but the interview was easier than I thought, just like chatting. The experience of the first interview made me braver to face more interviews in the future.

2023

Participated in the art camp, which was a camp organized by my sister. I participated in it during the form6 holidays. I met many juniors and discovered many different new things. In particular, I learned to listen to other people's ideas and respect them.

2022

After graduating from SPM, I worked for the first time. I woke up at 7 o'clock every day to work and got home at 8 o'clock in the evening. Although I met bad people, it was a very important social experience. It taught me to protect myself and be wary of others.

2021

My first time to organize a camp. I learned from the camps I had participated in before and started to consider various details and factors. I successfully organized a camp and let my juniors become our campers. It was a very important experience because my efforts were seen and I learned a lot.

2020

Affected by covid-19, I participated in the online camp for the first time. I listened to several lectures and played games online. Although it was difficult to play, I still met new friends and ended the online camp happily.

2019

I was the team leader for the first time. I led my own team in the camp, took care of the younger team members, played and trained with them. It was a happy memory.

2018

Participated in the sports meeting drill competition for the first time. I was a member of the police cadets. On that day, I wore the first uniform of the police cadets for the first time, which was a complete uniform.

2017

Participated in the first camp, which was the National Chinese Language Society Camp for Secondary Schools. It was the first time I stayed overnight outside and could not sleep, but I met a lot of good people. It was a precious memory and experience.

2016

Participated in a crosstalk performance. It was the first time I performed crosstalk in front of the whole class. I was very nervous, but the teacher praised me after the performance. I, who was always stage-frightened, successfully took the first step.
