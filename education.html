My Education Journey

SMJK Krian

I spent my time in SMJK Krian from Form 1 to Form 5. It is a school with a good school spirit and is an important memory for me.
The school motto of SMJK Krian is "Propriety, Righteousness, Honesty and Shame".
From this, I learned that propriety is the most important quality of a person and I respects the characteristics of everyone.

SMK Tunku Abdul Rahman(Form 6)

Since my secondary school did not have Form 6 courses, I came to SMK Tunku Abdul Rahman to continue my studies.
I benefited a lot from the Form 6 courses, and the most important thing I gained was my best friends and the teachers who were so kind to me.
This journey is very meaningful to me and has laid the foundation for my future.

Universiti Malaysia Perlis (UniMAP)

So far, I have come to University Malaysia Perlis, UniMAP. I am very grateful for the opportunity to come here, and I am also grateful for everything here, teachers, friends, everything. University life is beyond my expectations, but full of challenges. I have been exposed to many different things, learned things that I am not familiar with, and had more opportunities to exercise myself. These have gradually made me brave, dare to try, dare to fight, dare to refuse, and dare to dream.
And this reminds me of a saying: the future is long, and so are dreams.
